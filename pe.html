SUBJECT OVERVIEW

Rhythmic activities include any kind of activity thatis based upon a steady and prominent beat. During rhythmic activities individuals participate in rhythmic body movement, drumming, auditory stimulation, playing musical instruments, singing, and chanting,to name a few, with a trained leader guiding the group toward a therapeutic purpose.
